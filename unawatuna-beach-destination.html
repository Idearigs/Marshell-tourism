Unawatuna Beach

Golden crescent bay protected by coral reefs, offering perfect swimming, snorkeling, and stunning sunsets.

Relaxing Beach Destination

Experience the Golden Beauty of Unawatuna Beach

Discover Sri Lanka's most famous beach destination, where a perfect golden crescent meets coral reefs and historic charm. Unawatuna offers the ideal combination of natural beauty, vibrant marine life, and cultural heritage just minutes from historic Galle Fort.

About Unawatuna Beach:

Unawatuna Beach is celebrated as one of the world's most beautiful beaches, featuring a distinctive crescent-shaped bay with golden sand and calm turquoise waters. Protected by a coral reef that creates a natural swimming pool, this beach offers safe swimming conditions and excellent snorkeling opportunities.

Located just 6 kilometers from the UNESCO World Heritage site of Galle Fort, Unawatuna perfectly combines beach relaxation with cultural exploration. The beach has evolved into a vibrant destination that attracts visitors from around the globe while maintaining its authentic Sri Lankan coastal charm.

What Makes Unawatuna Special:

Unawatuna's protected coral reef creates an underwater paradise that's easily accessible from the shore. The reef is home to a diverse array of tropical fish, sea turtles, and colorful corals, making it one of Sri Lanka's best snorkeling spots. The calm waters created by the reef also make it ideal for swimming and water sports.

  • Snorkeling among vibrant coral reefs
  • Swimming in protected calm waters
  • Exploring historic Galle Fort nearby
  • Enjoying beachfront restaurants and cafes
  • Watching spectacular southern coast sunsets
Best Time to Visit

Unawatuna can be enjoyed year-round, but the best conditions are from November to April during the dry season. This period offers calm seas, excellent visibility for snorkeling, and minimal rainfall. The protected nature of the bay means swimming is generally safe throughout the year, though conditions are most favorable during these months.

For the best snorkeling experience, visit during the morning hours when visibility is at its peak and marine life is most active. The late afternoon also offers beautiful lighting for photography and stunning sunset views over the Indian Ocean.

  • Perfect golden crescent bay protected by coral reef - ideal for safe swimming
  • Excellent snorkeling with easy access to vibrant coral reefs and marine life
  • Close proximity to UNESCO World Heritage Galle Fort for cultural exploration
