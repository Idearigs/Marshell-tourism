Cool Relaxing Visit

  • 7 Days 6 Nights
  • Perfect for Relaxation
  • Hill Country & Temples, Sri Lanka

(Perfect for Wellness)

Journey Overview:

Escape to Sri Lanka's most tranquil and serene destinations on this 7-day relaxing journey. Experience the cool climate of the hill country, visit peaceful temples, explore botanical gardens, and unwind in the most beautiful and calming locations the island has to offer.

Detailed Itinerary:

01
Day 01: Arrival in Colombo

Welcome to Sri Lanka! Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, you'll be warmly welcomed and transferred to a peaceful hotel in Colombo. Spend the evening relaxing and preparing for your rejuvenating journey ahead.

02
Day 02: Visit Temple of Tooth, Kandy & Peradeniya Botanical Garden

Journey to the beautiful hill city of Kandy. Visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic, one of the most revered Buddhist temples. Spend a peaceful afternoon exploring the stunning Royal Botanical Gardens at Peradeniya, home to over 4,000 species of plants in a serene setting.

03
Day 03: Cool Relaxing at Nuwara Eliya

Travel to Nuwara Eliya, known as "Little England" for its cool climate and colonial charm. Relax in this hill station surrounded by tea plantations, enjoy the crisp mountain air, and take leisurely walks through the beautiful landscapes and gardens.

04
Day 04: Visit Mahamevnawa Buddhist Monastery at Ella & Nine Arch Bridge

Travel to the scenic mountain town of Ella. Visit the peaceful Mahamevnawa Buddhist Monastery for meditation and spiritual reflection. Explore the famous Nine Arch Bridge, an architectural marvel surrounded by lush greenery and tea plantations.

05
Day 05: Travel to Galle

Journey to the historic coastal city of Galle. Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Galle Fort, with its charming colonial architecture, narrow streets, and stunning ocean views. Relax by the sea and enjoy the peaceful coastal atmosphere.

06
Day 06: Visit Galle & Travel to Colombo

Continue exploring Galle with visits to its historic ramparts, museums, and quaint shops. In the afternoon, travel back to Colombo for your final evening. Enjoy a relaxing dinner and reflect on your peaceful journey through Sri Lanka's most serene locations.

07
Day 07: Departure

Your final day in Sri Lanka. After breakfast, you'll be transferred to the airport for your departure, carrying with you the peaceful memories and renewed spirit from your cool and relaxing journey through the beautiful island of Sri Lanka.

Perfect For:

This tour is specially designed for travelers seeking peace, tranquility, and spiritual renewal. Ideal for those who want to experience Sri Lanka's natural beauty, cultural heritage, and peaceful environments away from the hustle and bustle of city life.
